What technology does Threds use?

The virtual closets we create not only rely on technology, but also incredible women that are dedicated to ensuring that every item is fitted with care. While we do use machine learning to scale our processes, we make sure the human’s touch is not lost. Quality, inclusivity, and authenticity matter to us - and that can not be programmed.

Is Threds free?

Yes, Threds is 100% free.

When will the Threds app be available for download?

We are rolling out the app slowly. Sign up for our waitlist, and keep an eye out for your own exclusive download link.
